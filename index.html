Early Life

Born on January 28, 1985, in Frankfurt, Germany, J. Cole was raised in Fayetteville, North Carolina. He began rapping at age 12, and by the time he was 15, he had already started recording his own music.

Career Beginnings

J. Cole signed to Roc Nation in 2009, becoming the first artist to sign with Jay-Z's label. His debut album, *Cole World: The Sideline Story*, reached number one on the US Billboard 200 chart.
