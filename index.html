About J. Cole

About J. Cole

J. Cole was born on January 28, 1985, in Fayetteville, North Carolina.

He gained recognition with his mixtape The Warm Up and later signed with Roc Nation.

Achievements

  1. First rapper in history to go platinum with no features.
  2. Multiple Grammy nominations.
  3. Founder of Dreamville Records.
